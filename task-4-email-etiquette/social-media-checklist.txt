Social Media Do’s and Don’ts;

✅ Do’s:
 . Maintain a professional and respectful profile
 . Use privacy settings to protect personal information
 . Think before posting any content
 . Verify information before sharing
 . Use social media for learning and networking


❌ Don’ts:
. Do not share personal details like phone number or address
. Do not post offensive or inappropriate content
. Do not spread fake news or misinformation
. Do not overshare personal life
. Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
